Tier 1 - Base Resources
Cobalt, Chromium, Sulphur - early metals and chemical base
Sand, Silicon - construction and electronics foundation

Tier 2 - Fluids and Cooling
Water, Caldrine, Cryogel - cooling and early liquid mechanics

Tier 3 - Midgame Industrial
Obsidium, Electrum, Voltite - alloy, conductive material, and power-focused resource set

Tier 4 - Chemical Expansion
Pyroxene, Hydrogen, Chlorine, Carbon, Acid - first major gas/liquid chemistry tier

Tier 5 - Nuclear & Synthetic Materials
Plutonium, Plutolux, Aerogel - nuclear branch and advanced structural materials

Tier 6 - Biological & Reactive
Algae, Phosphor, Oxygen - biomass and oxidizer tier

Tier 7 - Refined Chemicals & Fuels
Chloride, Flash Fuel, Lubricant - industrial efficiency and combustion tier

Tier 8 - High-Temperature & Crystal Tier
Superheated Slag, Diamond - heat-based and ultra-dense refinement tier

Tier 9 - Exotic Energy Tier
Tritium, Plasma - final-stage fuels for fusion and endgame power generation
